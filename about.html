About

Welcome to our School Tour page! Our recent tour provided computer science students, myself included, with an extraordinary blend of education and exploration, taking them through Manila, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, and Baguio. In Manila, students visited top tech companies and prestigious universities, engaging in hands-on workshops and absorbing invaluable insights from industry leaders. These experiences ignited their passion for technology, honed their practical skills, and offered a vivid glimpse into their future careers in the tech world.

The journey was equally enriched by awe-inspiring scenic views and cultural experiences. In the Subic Bay Freeport Zone, students not only marveled at pristine beaches and lush landscapes but also visited innovative companies and workplaces, gaining firsthand experience of the thriving tech industry in the area. The adventure culminated in the cool, mountainous haven of Baguio, where breathtaking vistas and serene environments provided the perfect backdrop for reflection and growth. This unforgettable tour not only enhanced our students' technical prowess but also broadened their horizons, blending learning and leisure into a truly fabulous educational adventure.

Yours, Abdel-Khaliq Abdulla BSCS 3B s.y. 2023-2024
