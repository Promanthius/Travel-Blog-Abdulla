PAGASA Weather Station and First Aviation Academy, Subic Bay Freeport Zone (Day 2)

After our exploration of Subic Bay Freeport Zone, our journey continued with a visit to the PAGASA Weather Station, where we were treated to an exciting lecture on their operational techniques and the array of tools they employ. The station's dedication to forecasting and monitoring weather patterns left us with a deeper appreciation for meteorology. Adjacent to the station, we discovered the First Aviation Academy (FAA), a hub of aviation excellence. Here, we marveled at their fleet of aircraft and state-of-the-art flight simulators, which offered a hands-on glimpse into the world of aviation training. This experience was truly awe-inspiring, blending education with the thrill of exploring cutting-edge technology in a scenic and vibrant setting.
