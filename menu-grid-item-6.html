Strawberry Farm and Chinese Bell Church, Baguio (Day 6)

As our technical tour concluded, we eagerly transitioned to the recreational part of our journey, heading towards the breathtaking ranges of Baguio. Our first stop was the picturesque strawberry farm, where the vibrant, ripe strawberries seemed to paint the fields in hues of red and green. The air was crisp and refreshing, a perfect contrast to the bustling city life we had left behind. While we didn't pick strawberries ourselves, I indulged in a marvelous strawberry and avocado ice cream cone that was simply divine, blending the sweet and creamy flavors perfectly.

Next, we visited the enchanting Chinese Bell Church, an architectural marvel nestled amidst the serene hills. The intricate designs and tranquil atmosphere of the church were awe-inspiring, offering a moment of peace and reflection. The vibrant colors and ornate decorations of the temple were a feast for the eyes, each detail telling a story of rich cultural heritage. This part of our journey was not just a visual treat but a soulful experience, leaving us with cherished memories of Baguio's natural beauty and cultural treasures.
