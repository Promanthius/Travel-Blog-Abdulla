National Museum of The Philippines, Manila (Day 4)

During our visit to the National Museum of the Philippines, I initially questioned its relevance to our educational field. However, my perspective quickly shifted as I became mesmerized by the stunning artwork on display. While my preference leaned towards hand-drawn pencil art, every piece in the museum was undeniably beautiful and thought-provoking. The experience not only deepened my appreciation for artistic expression but also sparked contemplation about the intersection of creativity and technology in our modern world.
